// Our Story

About ThodaLocal

Ek 18 saal ke ladke ne socha — agar har badi company online aa sakti hai, toh chhoti local dukaan kyun nahi? Aur phir ThodaLocal bana.

// The Problem

Kya Problem Solve Kar Rahe Hain?

India mein lakho chhote local stores hain — sabzi wale, halwai, kirana stores, salons. Par inki koi online presence nahi. Log inhe dhundh nahi sakte. Trust nahi kar sakte. Aur yeh stores slowly band ho rahe hain.

Doosri taraf — users ko nahi pata ki unke area mein kaun sa store hygienic hai, safe hai, genuine hai. Koi guarantee nahi. Koi transparency nahi.

ThodaLocal yahi fix karta hai.

// The Solution

ThodaLocal Kya Karta Hai?

Har local store — chahe kitna bhi chota ho — ThodaLocal pe list ho sakta hai. Par sirf list hona kaafi nahi. Har store ko hamare hygiene verification se guzarna padta hai. Sirf verified stores ko badge milta hai.

Users live camera feed se dekh sakte hain apna order pack hote hue. 100% transparency. Aur hamare apne verified delivery network se order seedha ghar tak pahunchta hai.
