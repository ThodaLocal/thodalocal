Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Your privacy matters to us.

1. Who We Are

ThodaLocal is operated by RYC Corporation, based in Gurugram, Haryana, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform at thodalocal.in.

2. Information We Collect

When you use ThodaLocal, we may collect:

  • Store owners: Store name, owner name, phone number, area/locality
  • Customers: Name, email address, delivery address, area/locality
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, device information
  • Camera data: Live video feed from head-mounted cameras during active orders (not stored permanently)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Process store registrations and customer waitlist sign-ups
  • Contact you regarding your listing or order status
  • Conduct hygiene verification inspections
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Send important platform updates and announcements
  • Detect and prevent fraud or policy violations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

4. Live Camera Data

ThodaLocal's live camera feature allows customers to view order packing in real time. Regarding this data:

  • Live feeds are streamed in real time and are not permanently recorded or stored
  • Store owners consent to camera operation as part of their listing agreement
  • Camera access is limited to active order periods only

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard tools including Google Workspace. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies

ThodaLocal may use basic cookies and analytics tools to understand how users interact with our platform. These help us improve the experience for everyone. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@thodalocal.in

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • Google Workspace — for data storage and form submissions
  • GitHub Pages — for website hosting
  • Google Analytics — for usage analytics (anonymous)

Each of these services has their own privacy policies which govern their use of your data.

9. Children's Privacy

ThodaLocal is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, please contact us immediately.
